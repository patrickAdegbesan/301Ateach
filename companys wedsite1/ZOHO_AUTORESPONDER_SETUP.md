## Troubleshooting

**If autoresponder doesn't work:**
- Check that "Enable" is checked
- Verify the correct email group is selected
- Ensure "Duration" is set (not expired)
- Wait 5 minutes and try again (sometimes takes a moment to activate)

**If you need to edit:**
- Go to Autoresponder settings
- Click the **pencil icon** next to the autoresponder
- Make changes and click "Save"

**If you need to disable temporarily:**
- Uncheck the "Enable" box
- Click "Save"
